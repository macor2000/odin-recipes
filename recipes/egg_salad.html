Egg salad sandwich

Description

Delicious and easy to make sandwich.

Ingredients

  • A tablespoon of mayonaise
  • Two slices of bread
  • One or two eggs

Directions

  1. Boil some water
  2. Put eggs in water and let sit for 15 minutes
  3. Peel shell off of eggs. Put eggs in bowl
  4. Start toasting the bread
  5. Put mayo in bowl with eggs and mix
  6. Once toast is done you are good to go!
