Diamond pickaxe (from Minecraft)

Description

A diamond pickaxe from the game minecraft. The ultimate tool for mining and making portals.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks
  • 3 diamonds

Directions

  1. Break a tree with your fist.
  2. Make a wooden pickaxe
  3. Mine stone
  4. Make a stone pickaxe
  5. Mine iron
  6. Make a iron pickaxe
  7. Mine diamonds
  8. Make a diamond pickaxe

Have fun mining with your new pickaxe!
